What is the MyStuff folder?

The MyStuff folder is used to add characters, karts, music and more to the mod that isn't already included in the CTGP-7.

You can install any mod that other people have created and this guide will cover how to do so for Characters. There are plenty of mods of available on websites like Gamebanana.

This guide assumes that you already have a custom character you want to install, it is the users responibilty to assume that any mods they have downloaded are compatible with CTGP-7.

If you need additional help you are more than welcome to visit our Discord Server

For advanced users and developers or anything that may not be covered on this page you should visit the documentation on this link.

Where is the MyStuff folder located?

The MyStuff folder is located in the folder you've installed CTGP-7 on your SD Card, if you have installed CTGP-7 normally it should be located in SD:CTGP-7/MyStuff

How to install Custom Characters

This guide will be using a Hatsune Miku character created by Sedral to serve as an example, you can install the mod here if you need to follow closely, but this can be translated to any working custom character.


Step 0: Extract your mod and prepare it for installation.

Upon downloading a custom character it is likely a .zip file, extract the zip(s) you have and it should look similarly to the screenshots listed below.

After confirming you have the files ready for installation you can move on to the next step.


Step 1: Navigating to your MyStuff folder.

Turn off your 3DS and plug your 3DS SD card into your computer, open your SD Card in your computers file explorer and navigate to your MyStuff folder for your custom characters. SD://CTGP-7/MyStuff/Characters

Step 2: Copying your mod to the MyStuff folder.

Go back to where you've downloaded your mod on your computer and copy the folder containing the mod to your SD Card's Character folder SD://CTGP-7/MyStuff/Characters. Do not copy the .zip file, it will not work if you do this.

DO NOT COPY

What and where to copy

It is recommended you install one character mod at a time, in the case that one of those characters you install cause the game to crash you can diagnose problem mods easier.

You may now eject your SD Card from you computer safely and reinsert it into your 3DS, you may continue to the next step.


Step 3: Enable your custom character

Open CTGP-7 and wait untill the Title Screen loads, Press Select to open the modpacks menu. Go to "Game Settings -> Custom Character Manager" and find the character that your custom character replaces, for our example mod Miku goes over Lakitu. After applying your changes press B to exit this menu, you will be told you need to reboot and you should reboot your console.

If your character is not in this menu you have either placed your character folder in an incorrect location or the mod is not compatible with CTGP-7

Step 4: Finalizing steps.

After restarting CTGP-7 ensure that the character is in your character selection screen, go to any single player mode to confirm this. If your game crashes you likely have installed a bad custom character and will need to remove it, visit the CTGP-7 Discord for assistance if needed.

Trobleshooting

If you experience crashing after installing your character, you may have installed the character incorrectly or the mod is not compatible with CTGP-7, visit the CTGP-7 Discord for help and inform the mod creator that their mod may not be functioning correctly. It is safe to remove the character and re-patch the game.
